Our story

What began as a personal passion for texture and colour has grown into a boutique brand rooted in craftsmanship and generosity. Every order is made to bring joy — from the first sketch to the final finishing touches.

We believe in slow fashion: mindful creation, detailed stitching, and pieces that feel elevated yet easy to wear. The result is clothing that looks timeless and feels personal.
