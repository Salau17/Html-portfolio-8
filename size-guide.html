Measure with confidence

For handmade crochet, accurate measurements matter most. Capture your natural body shape and share measurements that match how you want the final piece to feel.

Bust / chest

Measure around the fullest part of your bust or chest with a soft tape, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

Waist

Find the narrowest part of your waist and wrap the tape snugly but not tight.

Hips

Measure where your hips are fullest, usually around the widest part of your seat.

Length

Measure from the highest shoulder point down to where you want the garment to sit.
